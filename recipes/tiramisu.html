Tiramisu

Recipes

Description

Tiramisu is a popular and classic italian dessert. Tiramisu consists of ladyfingers, a sweet breadstick, soaked in coffee, and covered in mascarpone cheese, which gives tiramisu its signature flavor.

Ingredients

  • Mascarpone Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Ladyfingers
  • Black Coffee or Espresso
  • Cocoa Powder

Steps

  1. Whisk the mascarpone cream for 45 seconds.
  2. In another bowl, separate the egg yolks and add sugar. Mix over a double boiler so your eggs are not raw.
  3. Pour the yolk-sugar mixture onto the whipped mascarpone and whisk for 45 seconds.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg whites until stiff peaks.
  5. Mix the whipped egg whites with the mascarpone mixture gently in three batches.
  6. Take the ladyfingers and dip in the coffee for as long as your preference.
  7. In a rectangluar dish, add a layer of the dipped ladyfingers.
  8. Cover with half of the mascarpone cream.
  9. Add another ladyfinger layer and mascarpone cream layer. You should have four layers.
  10. Cover and refrigerate the Tiramisu for at least four hours.
  11. Dust with cocoa powder and serve.
