Miso Soup

Recipes

Description

Miso soup is a comforting and nourishing traditional Japanese dish, made from a light yet rich broth. The ingredients are simple yet complex in synergy. Enjoyable at any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • Dashi - Japanese soup stock from kelp
  • Miso paste
  • Cubed Tofu
  • Dried wakame seaweed
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Making the Dashi stock from kombu, or dried kelp by soaking and heating it in water.
  2. Turn off the heat and add Miso paste, made from fermented soybean paste
  3. Not boiling the soup, add tofu to the soup.
  4. Heat the soup to a simmer yet not a boil, and add green onions when finished.
