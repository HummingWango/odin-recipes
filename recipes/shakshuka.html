Shakshuka

Recipes

Description

Shakshua is a North African baked egg dish where eggs are poached in a rich tomato & red pepper stew with Middle Eastern spices. It has many variations across countries such as Morocco, Tunisia, Algeria, and Egypt.

Ingredients

  • Large eggs
  • Red Onions
  • Garlic cloves
  • Tomatoes
  • Cumin, smoked paprika, and chili flakes
  • Basil or cilantro
  • Optional: Goat/Feta cheese to finish
