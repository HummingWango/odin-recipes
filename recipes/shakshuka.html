Shakshuka

Recipes

Description

Shakshua is a North African baked egg dish where eggs are poached in a rich tomato & red pepper stew with Middle Eastern spices. It has many variations across countries such as Morocco, Tunisia, Algeria, and Egypt.

Ingredients

  • Large eggs
  • Red Onions
  • Red and Yellow bell peppers
  • Garlic cloves
  • Tomatoes
  • Cumin, smoked paprika, and chili flakes
  • Basil or cilantro
  • Optional: Goat/Feta cheese to finish

Steps

  1. Using a cast iron pan, warm olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, sliced peppers and garlic, saut√©ing on low heat for 5-7 minutes
  3. Add all spices and salt, stir for 2 minutes
  4. Add tomato and water, simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Make "wells" in the tomato stew and crack the eggs into the wells, sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  6. Bake until the egg whites become cooked or 8-10 minutes.
  7. Garnish with cilantro or basil to finish.
